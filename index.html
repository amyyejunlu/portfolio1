About

Hi! I’m Amy. I studied fashion design at Parsons School of Design and worked as a fashion designer for five plus years.

The unique experience of working as a fashion designer greatly impacted me in how I think and approach problems as a designer. It taught me how to iterate fast, think creatively, solve problems, craft stories, and look for connections among seemingly disconnected places.

Design at its core is finding creative solutions to solve problems and the constant drive to do better. My curiosity and drive to design meaningful solutions propels me to constantly explore and examine new ideas and outcomes.
